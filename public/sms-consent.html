HostTurn SMS Notifications

HostTurn (hostturn.com) is a short-term rental cleaning company serving property owners and cleaning professionals in Vermont.

SMS Program Description

HostTurn sends operational text messages related to cleaning services. These messages are not marketing — they are transactional notifications directly related to active cleaning jobs.

Message types include:

  • Daily cleaning schedule and job assignments
  • Arrival and departure confirmations
  • Job completion notifications
  • Checkout time confirmations
  • Schedule change alerts

Who Receives Messages

HostTurn sends SMS messages to two groups:

  • Cleaning staff: Employees and contractors who are assigned to clean properties
  • Property owners/managers: Individuals who have active cleaning service agreements with HostTurn

How Consent Is Collected

Cleaning staff: Provide their mobile phone number and consent to receive work-related SMS messages during their employment or contractor onboarding process. Each team member is informed that HostTurn will send text messages about job assignments, schedules, and check-ins. They provide their phone number on their intake form and verbally agree to receive messages.

Property owners/managers: Provide their mobile phone number and consent to receive cleaning status text notifications when they enter into a service agreement with HostTurn. They are informed during the contracting process that they will receive SMS updates about cleaning activities at their properties.

All recipients receive an initial welcome message identifying HostTurn as the sender and confirming their enrollment in SMS notifications.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on cleaning activity. Cleaning staff may receive 1-5 messages per active cleaning day. Property owners typically receive 1-2 messages per cleaning event (confirmation and completion).

Opt-Out Instructions

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from HostTurn. After opting out, no further messages will be sent. To resume messages, text START to the same number.

Help

For help with HostTurn SMS notifications, reply HELP to any message or contact us at pedro@hostturn.com.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. HostTurn does not charge for SMS messages, but your mobile carrier may apply standard messaging rates.

Privacy

We will never share, sell, or distribute your mobile phone number or any mobile information to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
